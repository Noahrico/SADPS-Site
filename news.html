New Deputy Director of SADPS Appointed!

SAN ANDREAS - The San Andreas Department of Public Safety (SADPS) is proud to announce the official appointment of Rob Gucci as the new Deputy Director of the Department, effective immediately.

Deputy Director Gucci brings a welth of experience, a proven commitment to sublic service, and a forward-thinking approach to law enforcement leadership. In this role, he will serve as the second-in-command of the Department, working directly alongside the Director to oversee operational effectiveness, support agency leadership, and continue advancing the department's mission of service, safety, and professionalism across the state.

"Gucci has consistently demonstrated an unwavering dedication to public safety, strategic leadership, and fostering strong community relationships," said Nathan Linstead, Director of the San Andreas Department of Public Safety. "We are confident that this appointment will only strengthen our department's vision and drive toward excellence."

The San Andreas Department of Public Safety looks forward to the contributions of Deputy Director Gucci will make in his new role and remains committed to transparent, progressive, and community-oriented law enforcement.

Leadership Appointments Within State Public Safety Agencies

SAN ANDREAS - The San Andreas Department of Public Safety (SADPS) is proud to announce key leadership changes within its statewide public safety divisions.

Effective immediately, Deputy Commissioner Jordan Thompson has been appointed as the new Commissioner of the San Andreas Highway Patrol (SAHP) following the official resignation of Commissioner Jeffrey Wheezer. Commissioner Thompson will now assume full responsibilities for overseeing the day-to-day operations, strategic planning, and continued enforcement efforts of the Highway Patrol. His commitment to public safety, operational excellence, and community-focused policing has made him an invaluable asset to the department, and SADPS has full confidence in his ability to lead SAHP into the future.

In addition to this transition, the San Andreas Department of Public Safety is pleased to announce the appointment of Jimmy White as Chief of the San Andreas Department of Fire Rescue (SAFR). Chief White brings a wealth of experience in emergency response operations, fire safety, and leadership. His vision for a responsive, well-trained, and community-integrated fire and rescue service aligns with the mission and values of SAFR.

“These appointments reflect our ongoing commitment to placing strong, capable leaders at the forefront of our public safety agencies,” said Director Nathan Linstead, head of the San Andreas Department of Public Safety. “Both Commissioner Thompson and Chief White have demonstrated the professionalism, integrity, and forward-thinking leadership that our state needs.”

The San Andreas Department of Public Safety extends its appreciation to former Commissioner Wheezer for his years of service and leadership, and welcomes both Commissioner Thompson and Chief White into their new roles.
