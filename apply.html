Apply to Join SADPS

Serve with Purpose. Lead with Integrity. Apply Today. The San Andreas Department of Public Safety (SADPS) is actively recruiting professional, motivated, and dedicated individuals who are ready to step into a meaningful role in public service. Whether you're an experienced leader or just starting your journey, SADPS offers a wide range of opportunities for those who are committed to serving and protecting the communities of San Andreas.

As a unified public safety agency, SADPS is proud to oversee and operate multiple departments, each playing a vital role in ensuring the safety and well-being of our citizens. Applicants have the unique opportunity to explore and apply for a position in one of the following divisions:

San Andreas Department of Public Safety (SADPS) – The central leadership and administrative body guiding the vision and mission of all SADPS operations.

San Andreas Highway Patrol (SAHP) – Focused on traffic enforcement, highway safety, and specialized response units for high-speed and rural operations.

Sheriff's Office (SO) – Serving the counties and unincorporated areas with law enforcement, warrant execution, and jail operations.

Police Department (PD) – Responsible for urban policing, community engagement, and municipal crime prevention and enforcement.

San Andreas Communications Division (Dispatch) – The lifeline of emergency services, providing accurate, fast, and professional communication and coordination.

San Andreas Fire Rescue (SAFR) – Delivering life-saving fire, rescue, and emergency medical services across the state.

Joining SADPS means becoming part of a team built on trust, discipline, accountability, and respect. We seek individuals who are not only prepared to wear the uniform but also committed to upholding the highest standards of service and professionalism—on duty and off.

If you are ready to answer the call, face real challenges, and make a lasting impact, the San Andreas Department of Public Safety welcomes your application.

Take the first step. Apply now. Serve with honor.
