SADPS Resources & Documentation

The San Andreas Department of Public Safety (SADPS) makes use of a wide array of opperational resources and internal documentation to ensure the agency runs efficiently, safely, and with the highest level of professionalism. These resources serve as the backbone of our department's daily operations, guiding everything from training and accountability to personel management and public safety procedures.

Whether you are a current member of SADPS, a new recruit or a prospective applicant, these tools are designed to promote clarity, consistency, and excellence across all divisions. By adhering to these standards and utilizing these resources responsibly, we are able to maintain order, preparedness, and operational integrity across every level of the department.

Department Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

The SOP outlines the core policies, procedures, and operational expectations for all members of the San Andreas Department of Public Safety and its sub-agencies. It serves as the guiding document for professional conduct, field procedures, and administrative practices. Every member is responsible for knowing and following the SOP to ensure consistency, accountability, and integrity in all aspects of duty.

Department Roster(s)

Training handbooks offer structured guidance on the skills, knowledge, and procedures required to perform successfully in various public safety roles. From law enforcement techniques to emergency medical procedures, these handbooks are critical for preparing both new recruits and seasoned personnel. Regular review of training material ensures that all members operate with competence, safety, and professionalism.

Training Handbooks

Department rosters are essential tools that track active personnel, ranks, roles, and assignments within each division. They provide leadership and staff with a real-time overview of department structure, helping ensure efficient communication, proper chain-of-command, and accountability. Staying up-to-date on roster information is vital for maintaining operational readiness and organizational transparency.

Firearms Discharge Report

The Firearms Discharge Report is a critical accountability tool used whenever a service weapon is discharged, whether during training, enforcement, or use-of-force incidents. Submitting this report ensures transparency, promotes officer accountability, and supports internal reviews. It is mandatory that all firearm discharges be reported in a timely and thruthful manner to uphold public trus and departmental standards.
