Mission Statement

The mission of the San Andreas Highway Patrol is to uphold the law with integrity and protect the lives of all who travel our roadways. Committed to public safety and professional service, we strive to build trust through accountability, vigilance, and dedication.

Vision Statement

The vision of the San Andreas Highway Patrol is to be a trusted leader in law enforcement, setting the standard for safety, integrity, and service on our highways. We aim to create a safer future through innovation, community partnership, and a steadfast commitment to excellence.

Attention Citizens of San Andreas,

Are you ready to uphold the law and keep our highways safe? The San Andreas Highway Patrol is looking for dedicated and disciplined individuals to join our ranks and serve the people of this great state.

As a Trooper with SAHP, you will:

  • Patrol state highways and ensure traffic laws are enforced
  • Respond to emergencies and assist motorists in need
  • Participate in high-speed pursuits, traffic stops, and accident investigations
  • Work closely with local law enforcement agencies to maintain order

We are looking for applicants who:

  • Value integrity, professionalism, and teamwork
  • Have strong communication skills and the ability to make quick decisions
  • Are ready to undergo rigorous training and continuous evaluation

To apply:

Step up and prove your dedication. Submit your application today through the application form linked below. Be prepared for a thorough vetting process, including interviews and field training.

Join the San Andreas Highway Patrol - where your service makes a difference, and your actions protect the citizens and roadways of San Andreas.

Server with honor. Protect with pride.
