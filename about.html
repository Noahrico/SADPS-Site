About Us

The San Andreas Department of Public Safety (SADPS) proudly serves as the state’s leading law enforcement and regulatory agency. Our mission is clear: to protect life and property, enforce state laws, and ensure public safety through a combination of proactive education, consistent enforcement, and efficient emergency coordination.

As a statewide agency, SADPS oversees a broad network of specialized departments and public safety services that work together to serve and protect the people of San Andreas. Through comprehensive oversight, professional standards, and inter-agency collaboration, SADPS ensures a unified and effective approach to public safety across the state.

We are composed of the following divisions and departments:

San Andreas Department of Public Safety (SADPS Command Division) - The leadership and administrative backbone of the agency, responsible for strategic oversight, training standards, policy development, and coordination between all departments under our command.

San Andreas Highway Patrol (SAHP) - Tasked with traffic enforcement, accident investigation, highway safety, and high-speed interdiction across all major roadways and rural routes. SAHP also plays a critical role in statewide emergency response and tactical support.

Sheriff's Office (SO) - Provides law enforcement services to the county and rural communities, manages detention facilities, executes warrants, and operates specialized units such as K-9, Marine Patrol, and Search and Rescue.

Police Department (PD) - Focused on municipal law enforcement, crime prevention, urban patrol operations, and community policing. SAPD officers serve on the front lines in cities and towns throughout the state.

San Andreas Fire Rescue (SAFR) - Dedicated to protecting lives and property through firefighting, hazardous material response, technical rescue, and emergency medical services. SAFR also plays a key role in disaster response and public education on fire safety.

San Andreas Communications Division (Dispatch) - Serving as the nerve center for all public safety operations, the Communications Division ensures efficient and accurate dispatching of law enforcement, fire, and EMS units. This division is crucial for coordination and real-time response during critical incidents.

Special Enforcement Unit (SEU) - A highly trained tactical unit responsible for high-risk operations including warrant service, barricaded suspects, gang enforcement, fugitive apprehension, and counter-narcotics activities.

Licensing Regulatory Bureau - Oversees public safety-related licensing, including vehicle inspections, firearms permits, commercial driver licensing, and enforcement of regulatory compliance across state agencies and businesses.

Public Safety Communications & technology Division - Maintains and develops the technological infrastructure that supports SADPS operations statewide, including radio systems, CAD software, and digital evidence systems.

SADPS Patrol Divsion & Sub Division - In addition to overseeing its broader agencies, SADPS maintains its own active patrol division and specialized sub-units. These sub-divisions are structured to meet operational needs such as air support, commercial vehicle enforcement, training, recruitment, and professional standards.

At the core of SADPS is a dedicated team of professionals—officers, dispatchers, medics, support staff, and leadership—who bring experience, integrity, and a service-first mentality to everything they do. We are proud to hold ourselves to the highest standards of accountability and professionalism, working hand-in-hand with community members and allied agencies to promote a stronger, safer, and more resilient San Andreas.

Whether responding to emergencies, enforcing laws, conducting outreach, or protecting infrastructure, SADPS stands ready—24/7—to serve with honor.
